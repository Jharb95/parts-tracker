How to install it as an app on your phone
iPhone (Safari)

Open the URL in Safari (must be Safari, not Chrome)
Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
Name it "Parts Tracker" and tap Add
It now appears on your home screen like a real app

Android (Chrome)

Open the URL in Chrome
Tap the 3-dot menu
Tap "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App"
It now appears on your home screen

How data works

All data is stored locally in your browser (IndexedDB)
Each device has its own separate data
Photos are resized and stored in the browser (no size limit issues)
Use Export CSV to back up your text data
Use Import CSV to bring data from another device
Note: CSV export does not include photos (only text data)

Tips

Back up with Export CSV regularly
Photos taken with the camera are auto-resized to save space
The app works offline once loaded
On desktop, you see list + form side by side
On phone, you switch between Orders tab and New/Edit tab
